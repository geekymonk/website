Gaurav Kumar Agarwal

I am a third year PhD student in the department of Electrical Engineering at University of California, Los Angeles. My advisor is Prof. Christina Fragouli with whom I work on information theoretic security. Prior to my PhD, I did my masters in Electrical Communication Engineering at Indian Institute of Science Bangalore where I worked on distributed storage with Prof. P Vijay Kumar. I obtained my undergrad degree from Indian Institute of Technology, Roorkee.
